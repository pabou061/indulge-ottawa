About Indulge

Indulge Ottawa will feature all-inclusive sampling from a number of amazing restaurants, breweries, wineries, and additional specialty companies, to indulge every palate. Your entry includes all sampling and a reserved seat to enjoy the evening’s festivities (group bookings available).

The talented entertainment roster is quickly growing, with a popular local act in talks to provide music for the occasion. We’re building some extensive and exciting Live and Silent Auctions, hosted by Ryan Watson from Raising the Bid. Watson is known to delight a room, ensuring the live auction is a highlight of any event.
